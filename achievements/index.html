Achievements
Why we do it?
The team aims to provide exposure to all its members and thus participates in two reputed events that take place in USA and India respectively.
The events being:

 

BAJA SAE International
It is an international event that is organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE). Held in the United States of America (USA), it requires us to build an all-terrain, off-road vehicle intended for sale to off-roading enthusiasts. The event unites automotive experts from around the globe who share their technical expertise. The team has been participating every year since 2011.

Enduro Student India
The event with the same requirements as the BAJA SAE International is basically the Indian version of the international event. It showcases the talent that India has to offer in the automotive sector of the country.

KI Racing is a BAJA SAE team based in VIT University, Vellore. We are a bunch of creative engineering minds with the aim of taking the team to the topmost step of the podium. The team competes in the BAJA SAE Collegiate design series. Our job is to design and build a prototype of an All Terrain Vehicle (ATV) intended for sale to off-roading enthusiasts. About 30 undergraduate engineering students combine their skills and create a one-off design to be showcased in front of the whole world.
